Objective of the Experiment

 To study the signal conditioning circuit for RTD (Resistance Temperature Detector) by varying the temperature.

Overview

In this experiment you can choose the temperature from the thermometer in the range of 0 to 100 degree and observe the change in resistance of RTD .In addition, this experiment also shows the output of differential amplifier depending on different temperature you selected from 0 to 100 degree from thermometer.
